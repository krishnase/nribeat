What makes a fund backed by ex-OpenAI employees different from other AI VC funds?

Ex-OpenAI investors have shipped products at massive scale and understand technical depth. They'll back infrastructure and tools, not just applications. Not necessarily immediately. But if you're considering a move, prioritize opportunities at funded companies with:
