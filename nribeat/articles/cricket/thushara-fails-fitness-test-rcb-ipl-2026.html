Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What does it mean if a player fails the IPL fitness test?

It means the player doesn't meet BCCI's mandatory physical conditioning standards and cannot be granted permission (NOC) to play in the IPL. The franchise loses their investment, and the player must reapply or try again next season.

Q: Can Thushara take the fitness test again and join RCB?

Possibly, but it depends on BCCI's reassessment policies. If allowed, he'd need to retake and pass the test before the IPL 2026 season starts. RCB management will likely explore this option before settling for replacements.

Q: How does this affect India cricket squads for international matches?

Fitness failures at franchise level don't automatically exclude players from international cricket, but they raise red flags about conditioning. If a player can't meet IPL standards, selectors for India vs Australia Tests or other series might reconsider. Fitness is paramount in modern cricket.
