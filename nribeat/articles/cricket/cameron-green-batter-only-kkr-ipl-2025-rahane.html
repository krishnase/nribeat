What's Behind Green's Bowling Absence?

Rahane's Perspective: Concern or Opportunity?

The veteran batter sees Green's unavailability as a challenge—understandable, given that all-rounders who can bowl are premium assets in T20 cricket. With limited overs per match, losing a bowler who can also bat is like losing a Swiss Army knife from your toolkit.

However, Rahane also framed it pragmatically: as an opportunity for other bowlers to step up. That's the mark of smart leadership. Instead of dwelling on what KKR doesn't have, he's looking at who can fill the void.
