The Road Back: From Recovery to Readiness

Medical clearance is one thing; match fitness is another. Here's what Vlaeminck likely faced during her comeback journey:

Phase 1: Medical Recovery — Hospital care, pain management, initial physiotherapy focusing on basic range of motion and reducing inflammation.

Phase 2: Rehabilitation — Intensive physio to rebuild shoulder stability, rotator cuff strengthening, and proprioceptive training. This is where most athletes fail — the monotony is brutal.

Phase 3: Bowling-Specific Work — Gradual return to bowling actions without full intensity. Think short bowling drills, controlled pace, building confidence.

Phase 4: Match Simulation — Bowling in practice matches, facing real batsmen, experiencing the competitive pressure without the stakes.

Phase 5: Full Return — Competitive cricket with medical monitoring and gradual workload management.
