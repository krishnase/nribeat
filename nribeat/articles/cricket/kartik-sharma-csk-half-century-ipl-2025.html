Key Performers: The Rising Star and His Mentors

Kartik Sharma was undoubtedly the star of the night. Brought into CSK as a record signing, expectations were through the roof—and early season form had let him down. But on this night, against one of the toughest bowling units in IPL 2025, he showed why the franchise believed in him. What made this knock even more special? The influence of MS Dhoni and Stephen Fleming was visible in every stroke. Fleming, the legendary New Zealand captain now in CSK's coaching setup, has been working on the young batter's mindset and technique.

"There's a duty of care when you invest heavily in a youngster," Fleming told ESPNcricinfo after the match. And you could see that patience and belief paying dividends. Dhoni, watching from the commentary box, smiled knowingly—the boy was finally getting it.
