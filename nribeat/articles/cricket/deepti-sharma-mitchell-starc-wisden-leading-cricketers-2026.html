Let's talk about Deepti Sharma specifically, because she's earned every bit of this recognition. Over the past 12-18 months, Deepti has been:

  • A consistent run-scorer in ODIs and T20Is

  • A leader in the Indian women's cricket ecosystem

What makes Deepti special is that she's not a specialist—she's a true all-rounder who can rescue a collapsing batting lineup or take crucial wickets when the pressure's on. In the women's game, all-rounders of her caliber are rare and invaluable.

Mitchell Starc: Why Australia's Pace Spearhead Ranks Among the World's Best

Mitchell Starc has been the guy you watch when India faces Australia in big tournaments. His left-arm pace, ability to bowl yorkers at will, and record in ICC tournaments make him one of the most dangerous fast bowlers in the world. Whether it's Test cricket, ODIs, or T20s, Starc has the skill to dismantle batting lineups on any given day.
