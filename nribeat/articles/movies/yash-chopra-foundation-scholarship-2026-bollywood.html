Yash Chopra Foundation Scholarship 2026: Rs. 5 Lakhs for Film Industry Children

The Yash Chopra Foundation (YCF), the philanthropic wing of Yash Raj Films, has officially launched the second edition of its prestigious YCF Scholarship Program 2026 for the academic year 2026–27. This initiative provides substantial financial assistance of up to Rs. 5 lakhs per student, aimed at supporting higher education aspirations for children from Hindi film industry families. As the Indian entertainment industry continues to grow—with new Bollywood OTT releases and streaming content dominating platforms like Netflix India—this scholarship reinforces the industry's commitment to nurturing the next generation.

What Is the YCF Scholarship 2026?

The Yash Chopra Foundation Scholarship is a merit-based financial assistance program designed to ease the educational burden on families working behind and in front of the camera in Hindi cinema. Whether a parent works as a cinematographer, sound engineer, costume designer, or in any other capacity within the film industry, this scholarship recognizes their contribution to Indian cinema while supporting their children's academic growth.
