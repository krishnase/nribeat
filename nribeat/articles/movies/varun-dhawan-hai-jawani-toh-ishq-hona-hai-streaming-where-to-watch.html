Varun Dhawan's Hai Jawani Toh Ishq Hona Hai: New Bollywood Comedy Coming in 2025

Varun Dhawan is gearing up to deliver laughs in Hai Jawani Toh Ishq Hona Hai, a brand-new Bollywood comedy that's generating major buzz in the industry. The film marks an exciting collaboration between TIPS Films, PVRInox, and Cinepolis for all-India distribution—a strategic partnership that signals big theatrical ambitions for this project. Directed by David Dhawan (marking his 46th directorial venture), this mad-cap comedy reunites one of Bollywood's favorite father-son duos and promises to be a must-watch for comedy lovers among the Indian diaspora in the US.

The Story: What to Expect

While plot details remain largely under wraps, Hai Jawani Toh Ishq Hona Hai is being positioned as a hilarious, high-energy comedy that taps into the signature style David Dhawan is known for. The film stars Varun Dhawan in the lead role, with Pooja Hegde and Mrunal Thakur joining the ensemble cast. This trio brings together fresh chemistry and star power that's expected to light up screens when the film releases.
