What's Happening: The No-Phones Policy Explained

Love & War has become synonymous with secrecy in Bollywood circles. The strict measures reportedly include:

  • Complete prohibition of personal mobile devices on set

  • Limited behind-the-scenes content shared publicly

The Story: What to Expect (No Spoilers)

While plot details remain tightly under wraps, what we know is this: Love & War marks Sanjay Leela Bhansali's first directorial project in several years and reunites him with Alia Bhatt, his Gangubai Kathiawadi collaborator. The film is said to explore complex themes of love, conflict, and human relationships through Bhansali's signature grandiose storytelling style.

The casting of three leading men—Ranbir Kapoor, Vicky Kaushal, and a third actor—alongside Alia Bhatt suggests a multifaceted narrative structure. Given Bhansali's penchant for period dramas and opulent productions, expect lavish cinematography, intricate costume design, and music that could define the year.
