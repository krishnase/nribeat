Haunted: Echoes Of The Past – New Bollywood Horror Movie 2026

Bollywood horror filmmaker Vikram Bhatt is bringing back his iconic supernatural franchise with Haunted: Echoes Of The Past, set to release on June 12, 2026. The film—originally titled Haunted – Ghosts Of The Past—underwent a name change based on Bhatt's belief in cinematic superstition. In an exclusive interview, the director revealed that the title was altered because "you can't make a ghost film without believing in superstition." This highly anticipated sequel follows the massive success of Haunted 3D (2011), which revolutionized Bollywood's 3D horror landscape and became a sleeper hit.
