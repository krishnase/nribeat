The Story: What to Expect from The Traitors India Season 2

The Traitors is a Dutch reality format that has taken the world by storm, with successful adaptations in the US, UK, Australia, and beyond. The show's core premise is psychological brilliance: a group of contestants compete for a cash prize, but a select few are secretly designated as "Traitors" tasked with eliminating other players ("Faithful") without getting caught. The remaining Faithful must identify who the Traitors are before the final episode.
