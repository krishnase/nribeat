Kartik Aaryan's Naagzilla Postponed to Valentine's 2027

📅 April 15, 2026 ⏱ 5 min read ✍️ NRIBeat Editorial Source: Bollywood Hungama ↗

Kartik Aaryan's much-anticipated fantasy comedy Naagzilla has officially been postponed from its original August 14, 2026 release date and will now hit theatres during the Valentine's Day weekend in 2027. The film, backed by the powerhouse duo of Dharma Productions and Mahaveer Jain Films, was initially scheduled to coincide with the lucrative Independence Day weekend slot — a prime time for major Bollywood releases. However, makers have decided to reschedule the project to capitalize on the romantic Valentine's weekend instead, signaling a strategic shift in the film's release strategy.

The Story / What to Expect

While details remain under wraps, Naagzilla is being positioned as a fantasy-comedy that promises to deliver something unique to Indian audiences. The film marks another exciting collaboration between Kartik Aaryan and Dharma Productions, one of Bollywood's most consistent production houses known for launching innovative content across multiple genres.
