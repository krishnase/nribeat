Call Me Bae Season 2: Shruti Haasan Joins Ananya Panday in Thailand

The biggest update about Call Me Bae Season 2 just dropped: Shruti Haasan is joining the cast as Ananya Panday's new "behen" (sister), and they're currently filming in Thailand. This unexpected pairing of two powerhouse actresses has already sparked major excitement among fans of the hit Netflix India original, with audiences eager to see this fresh on-screen dynamic unfold when the new episodes premiere later this year.

## The Story / What to Expect

For those catching up: Call Me Bae is Netflix's stylish and witty series that follows Bae Teravati, a pampered Delhi heiress whose life takes a dramatic turn when she finds herself alone and broke in Mumbai. The first season was a massive hit with the Indian diaspora in the US and worldwide audiences, blending fashion, friendship, and heartfelt moments with laugh-out-loud comedy.
