Rajkummar Rao Turns 'Kanjoos' in Netflix's Toaster: Dark Comedy Premieres April 15

Netflix is dropping a hilarious dark comedy that proves one man's obsession with saving money can spark absolute chaos. Rajkummar Rao and Sanya Malhotra headline Toaster, Netflix's latest India original that's set to premiere on April 15, 2026. The newly released trailer shows Rao playing a penny-pinching character whose money-saving antics spiral into unexpected mayhem. If you're searching for new Bollywood movies streaming on Netflix, this is one dark comedy you won't want to miss.

The Story / What to Expect

Directed by Vivek Das Chaudhary and produced by Patralekhaa under Kampa Film, Toaster is a unique blend of humor and chaos that centers on one seemingly ordinary household appliance. The premise may sound simple, but the trailer reveals that this isn't your average domestic comedy—it's a dark, twisted take on what happens when a man's extreme frugality meets real-world consequences.
