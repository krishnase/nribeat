Filmmaker Hansal Mehta is launching a groundbreaking AI-led culinary series called Khana Dil Se: An AI Journey Through India's Kitchen in 2025. The innovative episodic series marks a unique collaboration between Collective Studios and True Story Films, blending India's rich food heritage with cutting-edge artificial intelligence technology. If you've been searching for new Bollywood movies and premium Indian OTT content, this ambitious project offers something entirely different from traditional streaming fare.

The Story / What to Expect

Khana Dil Se isn't your typical cooking show or food documentary. Instead, it's a conceptual journey through India's culinary landscape viewed through the innovative lens of artificial intelligence. Hansal Mehta, known for critically acclaimed films like Shahid and The Girl in the Spider's Web, returns to unexplored creative territory by examining how AI can help us understand and appreciate India's kitchen traditions.
